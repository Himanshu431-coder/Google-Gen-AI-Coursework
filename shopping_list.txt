Shopping List:

For the Tofu:
- 14 oz extra-firm tofu
- Olive oil
- Soy sauce or tamari
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder
- Black pepper

For the Bowl:
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup mixed greens or chopped romaine lettuce
- 1/2 cup shelled edamame (fresh or frozen)
- 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
- 1/2 cup cucumber
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 cup red cabbage
- Green onions

For the Sesame-Ginger Dressing:
- Soy sauce or tamari
- Rice vinegar
- Sesame oil
- Fresh ginger
- Garlic
